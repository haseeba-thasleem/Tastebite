Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfinger biscuits, rich mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder. It is soft, creamy, and perfectly balanced with coffee and chocolate flavors.

‚è± Prep: 20 mins
‚ùÑ Chill: 4 hrs
üç∞ Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 200 g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup strong coffee (cooled)
  • 250 g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • ¬Ω cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp chocolate shavings

Instructions

  1. Whip the fresh cream until fluffy.
  2. Add mascarpone, sugar, and vanilla. Mix gently.
  3. Dip ladyfingers quickly into the coffee.
  4. Arrange a layer in a serving dish.
  5. Spread a layer of cream mixture on top.
  6. Repeat layers until finished.
  7. Dust cocoa powder on top.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  9. Garnish with chocolate shavings before serving.
