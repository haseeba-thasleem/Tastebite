Indian Thali

Indian Thali is a complete meal that includes a variety of dishes like rice, roti, curry, dal, vegetables, chutney, curd, and dessert. It offers a perfect balance of taste, nutrition, and traditional Indian flavors in one plate.

üî• Cook: 50 mins

Ingredients

For Rice & Roti
  • 2 cups rice
  • 6 chapatis / rotis
For Dal
  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt to taste
For Veg Curry
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
For Sides
  • Curd
  • Pickle
  • Chutney
  • Papad
  • Sweet (gulab jamun / payasam)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and keep warm.
  2. Prepare rotis or chapatis.
  3. Boil dal with turmeric and salt.
  4. Prepare tadka with mustard and garlic and mix into dal.
  5. Cook vegetables with onion, tomato, and spices.
  6. Arrange rice, roti, dal, and curry on a plate.
  7. Add curd, pickle, chutney, and papad.
  8. Serve with sweet as dessert.
