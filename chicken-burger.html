Zinger Chicken Burger is a crispy fried chicken burger with spicy seasoning, fresh lettuce, and creamy mayo, served inside soft burger buns. A perfect fast-food style meal for burger lovers!

üî• Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

For Chicken
  • 4 chicken breast fillets
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
For Coating
  • 1¬Ω cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
For Burger
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix flour with spices for coating.
  3. Coat chicken well in flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil and deep fry chicken until golden and crispy.
  5. Toast burger buns lightly.
  6. Spread mayonnaise on buns.
  7. Place fried chicken on bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  9. Close with top bun.
  10. Serve hot with fries.
