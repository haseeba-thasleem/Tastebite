Dindigul Mutton Biryani

Dindigul Mutton Biryani is a famous South Indian delicacy known for its bold spices, juicy mutton and unique seeraga samba rice. This flavorful biryani is slow-cooked to perfection, giving you a rich, spicy and aromatic experience in every bite.

Ingredients

For Biryani
  • 500g mutton (with bones)
  • 2 cups seeraga samba rice
  • 3 tbsp oil + 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 3 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ¬Ω cup fresh curd
  • 1 bunch mint leaves
  • 1 bunch coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Spices
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak seeraga samba rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and ghee in a large pot.
  3. Add whole spices and saut√© till aromatic.
  4. Add sliced onions and fry till golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  7. Add chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala.
  8. Add mutton and mix well.
  9. Add curd, mint and coriander leaves.
  10. Cook for 10 minutes until mutton is coated well.
  11. Add soaked rice and required water.
  12. Cover and cook on low flame till rice is done.
  13. Mix gently and rest for 10 minutes.
  14. Serve hot with raita or salna.
