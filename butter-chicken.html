Naan & Butter Chicken is a classic North Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy, served with soft buttery naan. A perfect meal for special lunches and dinners.

Ingredients

For Butter Chicken
  • 500g boneless chicken
  • Â½ cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • Â½ cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
For Naan
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â½ cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Butter for brushing

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until lightly golden.
  3. In another pan, melt butter and add ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add cooked chicken and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add cream and mix gently.
  7. For naan, mix flour, yogurt, baking powder, and salt.
  8. Knead into soft dough and rest for 30 minutes.
  9. Roll and cook on hot pan until bubbles form.
  10. Brush naan with butter.
  11. Serve butter chicken hot with naan.
