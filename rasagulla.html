Rasgulla

Rasgulla is a classic Indian sweet made from soft paneer balls soaked in light sugar syrup. This dessert is famous for its spongy texture and juicy sweetness, making it loved by everyone.

üî• Cook: 25 mins

Ingredients

For Paneer Balls
  • 1 litre full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornflour
For Sugar Syrup
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cardamom pods

Instructions

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to curdle it.
  2. Strain and wash the paneer.
  3. Knead paneer with cornflour until smooth.
  4. Make small smooth balls.
  5. Boil sugar and water to make syrup.
  6. Add paneer balls to boiling syrup.
  7. Cover and cook for 15 minutes.
  8. Let them cool and absorb syrup.
  9. Serve chilled or warm.
