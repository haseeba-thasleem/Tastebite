Rice & Fish Curry is a comforting traditional dish made with fresh fish simmered in a spicy, tangy gravy and served with hot steamed rice. A perfect lunch meal that brings authentic South Indian flavors to your plate.

Ingredients

For Fish Curry
  • 500g fish pieces (seer fish / king fish)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind extract
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1¬Ω tbsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
For Rice
  • 2 cups raw rice
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and cook rice with water and salt. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard and fenugreek seeds.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder and coriander powder.
  6. Add tamarind extract and let the gravy boil.
  7. Add fish pieces carefully into the curry.
  8. Cook on low flame until fish is cooked.
  9. Add curry leaves and switch off the flame.
  10. Serve hot fish curry with steamed rice.
