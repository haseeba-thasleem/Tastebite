Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado, remove the seed, and scoop out the flesh.
  3. Mash the avocado in a bowl using a fork.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  5. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  6. Spread the avocado mixture on the toasted bread.
  7. Drizzle olive oil if desired.
  8. Sprinkle chilli flakes for extra flavor.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy.
