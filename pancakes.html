Instructions

  4. Heat a pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter and cook until bubbles appear.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Repeat for remaining batter.
  8. Stack pancakes on a plate.
  9. Drizzle generously with honey.
  10. Add butter or fruits if desired and serve hot.
